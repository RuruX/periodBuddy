# SOURCE: NHS
# TOPIC: Period problems

## What are period problems
Period problems are common and include heavy bleeding, painful periods, irregular periods, or missing periods.

## Heavy periods
Heavy periods mean losing a lot of blood during your period.
Symptoms include bleeding through clothes or needing to change pads often.

## Painful periods
Painful periods are cramps in the lower abdomen.
They often start when bleeding begins and last 2 to 3 days.

# TOPIC: Menstrual health

## What causes period pain
Period pain happens when the uterus contracts to shed its lining.

## When to see a doctor
You should see a doctor if period pain is severe, bleeding is very heavy, or periods change suddenly.
